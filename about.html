About Us

Tea Party Milk Tea, located in the heart of Zone-2, Poblacion, El Salvador, Philippines, offers a diverse and inviting menu for culinary enthusiasts. The establishment boasts an array of dimsum delights, including the popular siomai and tempura, as well as a delectable siomai-tempura combo. Crispy fries lovers can indulge in options like cheesy fries and the decadent fries overload. Aromatic iced coffee choices such as Caramel Macchiato and Salted Caramel Macchiato, along with the refreshing Strawberry Latte, are available to rejuvenate your senses. For a refreshing alternative, the menu features a Honey Lemon 22oz juice that is both invigorating and satisfying. The milktea selection is a highlight, offering exotic blends like CheesyMango and Sakura Strawberry, catering to a variety of tastes. Nachos enthusiasts can dive into the indulgent world of overloaded nachos, while those seeking warmth can enjoy the creamy and spicy goodness of the Ramen. For inquiries or orders, customers can conveniently contact Tea Party Milk Tea at 0967 842 9882 or via email at teapartymilktea@gmail.com. At Tea Party Milk Tea, each sip and bite promise a unique story of flavor and finesse, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience for all patrons.
